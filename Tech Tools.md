#Ethical Hacker gadgets from the [Ryan Montgomery Shawn Ryan Show video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X51d06g4tuk)

- **Flipper Zero**: Portable multi-tool for pentesters and geeks, used for RF, RFID, and custom firmware attacks like car key fob rolling codes. Official site: https://flipperzero.one
- **Dual ESP by Awok Dynamics**: Wi-Fi and Bluetooth add-on for Flipper Zero with GPIO pins, enabling attacks like deauth, evil portals, and Sour Apple Bluetooth spoofing. Official site: https://awokdynamics.com
- **BLE Shark Nano**: Compact wireless tool for captive portal attacks, creating fake Wi-Fi networks like "free Wi-Fi" to capture credentials via phishing pages. Official site: https://infishark.com
- **Glacier Secure Internet Device**: Cellular-based secure hotspot working in 130+ countries, providing encrypted connections to avoid public Wi-Fi risks; from Glacier, which also offers hardened phones. Official site: https://glacier.chat
- **Ray Hunter Firmware**: Custom firmware for Verizon Orbit hotspot (e.g., RC400L), detects stingrays (IMSI catchers) via green/red line indicator for phone tracking awareness. Install guide: https://efforg.github.io/rayhunter
